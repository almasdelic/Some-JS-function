FAQ



What are the editions and sizes?


Most pieces are editions of only 7 prints available in 3 sizes. For example, the unframed sizes for the square images are 18x18 inch, 30x30 inch, 40x40 inch. A signed and numbered Certificate of Authenticity is provided with your print for your records.


What is the price?


Pricing is set according to image demand/availability and is based on the desired size. Please inquire for exact details. Bundle pricing is also available for orders of multiple prints.


Is shipping available in my country?


Shipping is available worldwide. Unframed prints will ship directly from our professional photo lab in Los Angeles in dent resistant tubes..


Do they come framed?


If you are located in Southern California, expert framing is available. If you are located elsewhere, we will provide assistance to find the right framer for you in your area. Shipping fully framed prints carries a risk of damage in transit.


Can I purchase with Cryptocurrency?


Yes. We are now accepting Bitcoin, Ethereum, and USDC as payment. Please inquire about purchasing your print with crypto.
